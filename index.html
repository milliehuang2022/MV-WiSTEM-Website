    Welcome to Monta Vista WiSTEM Club!
    Meetings every other Wednesday @ B108

    STEM

    /stem

    noun

    1. the main body or stalk of a plant or shrub, typically rising above ground but occasionally subterranean.

    2. an amazing, supportive community of young women who come together to pursue their aspirations in science, technology, engineering, and math!

    Since 2012, MV WiSTEM has been a strong network in which young women feel encouraged to pursue a wide variety of STEM interests. Our club has explored many fields in the past like astronomy, artificial intelligence, environmental science, and many more! We also conduct science labs, projects, career panels, and other fun events in our bi-weekly meetings.

    So are you looking for more STEM opportunities? Or help in any of your classes? How about a positive environment to expand your passions in STEM? Join us every other Wednesday at B108! Feel free to contact us at mv.wistem@gmail.com if you have any questions :)
